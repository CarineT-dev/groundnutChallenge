How to make groundnut soup

Groundnut soup is a much appreciated local Cameroonian dish; it is generally eaten with rice or tubers such as yam.

ingredients

  • Groundnut
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Meat
  • Salt

preparation

  1. Fry and blend Groundnut.
  2. Blend together tomatoes, ginger and garlic ans set aside.
  3. Cook meat 30mins in an oven with a little salt.
  4. Poor a little olive oil in a cooking pot, wait about a minute and poor sliced onion for about 30 seconds.
  5. Add the blended tomatoes mixture to the pot and let cook 5 minutes.
  6. add about 1.5 litres of water and salt, cover the pot until it reaches boiling point.
  7. At boiling point, open and add blended Groundnut and let the pot open.
  8. Ler reduce for about 15 minutes.
  9. Put out the fire and cover the pot.
  10. Serve with rice or tubers.
